# BROKE-N-BORED's Blackjack Rules (Improved)

## 💰 Bankroll & Session Rules
1. **Buy in with what you're willing to lose.** That's your session bankroll — no ATM trips.
2. **Minimum bet = 1/20th of your bankroll.** ($10 min on a $200 session.)
3. **Walk away when you double your buy-in.** No exceptions.
4. **Walk away when you lose your buy-in.** No exceptions.
5. **Never chase losses.** If you hit your loss limit, the session is over.

## 🎯 Betting System (Win Progression — Replaces Martingale)
1. Start at your minimum bet.
2. **When you win:** increase your next bet by 50% (round to nearest chip). ($10 → $15 → $20 → $30)
3. **When you lose:** reset immediately to your minimum bet.
4. **After 3 consecutive wins:** lock in profit, reset to minimum.
5. You're riding hot streaks for profit, not chasing cold streaks into a hole.

## ✋ When to HIT
- **Always hit** hard 8 or below (no matter what the dealer shows)
- **Hit 9** against dealer 2 or 7–Ace
- **Hit 10** against dealer 10 or Ace
- **Hit 11** against dealer Ace
- **Hit 12** against dealer 2, 3, or 7–Ace
- **Hit 13–16** against dealer 7–Ace

## 🛑 When to STAND
- **Stand 12** against dealer 4, 5, or 6
- **Stand 13–16** against dealer 2–6
- **Stand 17+** always — never hit 17 or higher

## ⬇️ When to DOUBLE DOWN
- **Double 9** against dealer 3–6
- **Double 10** against dealer 2–9
- **Double 11** against dealer 2–10
- If the table won't let you double, just hit instead.

## ✂️ When to SPLIT
- **Always split Aces** — no exceptions
- **Always split 8s** — no exceptions
- **Split 2s, 3s** against dealer 4–7
- **Split 6s** against dealer 3–6
- **Split 7s** against dealer 2–7
- **Split 9s** against dealer 2–6 and 8–9 (stand against 7, 10, Ace)
- **NEVER split 4s, 5s, or 10s** — a 20 is gold, don't break it

## 🃏 Soft Hands (Ace counted as 11)
- **Soft 13–15 (A+2 through A+4):** Hit against everything
- **Soft 16–17 (A+5, A+6):** Double against dealer 4–6, otherwise hit
- **Soft 18 (A+7):** Stand against dealer 2, 7, 8. Double against 3–6. Hit against 9, 10, Ace.
- **Soft 19+ (A+8 or better):** Always stand

## 🚫 Table Selection Rules
- **Only play 3:2 blackjack tables** — walk away from any 6:5 table
- Prefer tables where the dealer STANDS on soft 17
- Prefer tables that allow doubling after splits

## 🧠 Discipline Reminders
- No gut feelings. Follow the chart every single time.
- No insurance bets — ever. It's a sucker bet.
- No side bets (Perfect Pairs, 21+3, etc.) — house edge is 3–10x worse.
- If you're tired, tilted, or drinking too much — walk away.
- Consistency beats creativity. The math only works if you never deviate.
